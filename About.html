About ULounge

  • ULounge is here to help enhance the campus experience for all students and tighten the campus community at UNC Charlotte.
  • ULounge is designed to help students find friends in their community and increase social interaction on campus as well as increase school spirit.
  • Students have the ability to learn about events and activities occurring on campus through student posts. Anyone can post, anyone can interact.
  • Make the most of your time on Campus, get out, get active, and HAVE FUN!

Terms of Service

  • All students with a valid UNCC email are eligible to use ULounge
  • Cursing, hate speech, or discussion of illegal activity will not be tolerated at ULounge
  • If any of the above guidelines are violated, users will receive ONE warning. If guidelines are violated again, the users account will be TERMINATED
